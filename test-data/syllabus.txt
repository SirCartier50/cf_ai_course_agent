LATE WORK POLICY
Assignments submitted late will lose 10% per day, up to a maximum of 3 days late. After 3 days, the assignment receives a zero. Each student is granted ONE free 3-day extension per semester, no questions asked — email the professor before the deadline to use it.

ATTENDANCE POLICY
Lecture attendance is not mandatory but strongly encouraged. Lab attendance is mandatory and counts toward the participation grade. Missing more than 2 labs without a valid excuse will result in a zero for the participation component. If you must miss a lab, email the professor at least 24 hours in advance.

ACADEMIC INTEGRITY
All submitted work must be your own. You may discuss concepts with classmates, but sharing code or copying solutions is strictly prohibited. First offense: zero on the assignment and a written warning. Second offense: F in the course and referral to the Dean of Students. Using AI tools (ChatGPT, Copilot, etc.) is allowed for learning and debugging, but you must write the final submitted code yourself. Copy-pasting AI-generated solutions is considered a violation.

MAKEUP EXAM POLICY
Makeup exams are only given for documented medical emergencies or university-approved absences. You must notify the professor at least 48 hours before the exam, or within 24 hours after in case of emergency. Documentation is required in all cases.

EXTRA CREDIT
There is no extra credit offered in this course. Focus on completing all assignments and doing well on exams.

ACCOMMODATIONS
Students with documented disabilities should contact the Disability Resource Center (DRC) and provide their accommodation letter to the professor within the first two weeks of class. The professor will work with the DRC to ensure appropriate accommodations are made.
